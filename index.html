About Me

  • I am a couture hat designer and maker
  • I am also passionate about sustainability
  • I run the 'Clothes Clinic' to teach people how to repair their clothes
  • I was born in East Berlin in 1958
  • I worked with David Shilling and Philip Treacy
  • I have also sold and made silk paintings, decoupage chairs, christmas cards among many things

About My Business

  • I make hats and fascinators for a range of events:
  • From wedding fascinators
  • To everyday bowler hats
  • I also run clothing repair workshops to teach people how to repair their own clothes
  • I can also run millinery workshops on demand

The Clothes Clinic

  • I run the 'Clothes Clinic' to teach people how to repair their clothes
  • I am passionate about sustainability
  • I did my MA in Fashion at Goldsmiths and we thought about Fast Fashion
  • Ever since then I had wanted to make a difference
  • The 'Clothes Clinic' runs twice a month at the Crabtree & Evelyn Townhouse
  • There I teach locals how to repair their clothes
  • I also run a workshop called 'Beyond Repair' in which I show people how to refashion used denim into stylish fascinators and hairbands
